# Project Proposal

## Project Title

**JobShield – AI-Powered Job Scam Detection Platform**

---

# 1. Project Overview

JobShield is an AI-powered web application designed to help job seekers identify and avoid fraudulent job opportunities. The platform analyzes job descriptions, recruiter emails, interview invitations, and hiring messages using Artificial Intelligence to estimate the likelihood of a scam. It also verifies company information, assists users in validating recruiters, and provides a community-driven reporting system for fraudulent job postings.

The project aims to reduce financial fraud and protect job seekers by offering an intelligent, explainable, and user-friendly verification platform.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

With the rapid growth of online recruitment platforms, fake job offers have become increasingly common. Scammers exploit job seekers by posting fraudulent vacancies, impersonating recruiters, requesting registration or training fees, and collecting sensitive personal information.

Fresh graduates and job seekers are particularly vulnerable because they often lack experience in identifying fraudulent recruitment practices.

Currently, users must manually verify companies and recruiters across multiple websites, making the process time-consuming and unreliable. There is no centralized platform that combines AI-powered scam analysis, company verification, recruiter validation, and community reporting.

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

JobShield provides a centralized platform where users can submit job-related content for automated analysis. The system evaluates multiple risk indicators, verifies company information, analyzes recruiter email domains, and generates an explainable risk assessment rather than making absolute claims.

The platform also enables users to report suspicious recruiters and fraudulent companies, helping build a collaborative database of known scams.

---

# 4. Objectives

* Develop an AI-assisted platform for identifying suspicious job offers.
* Verify companies using publicly available official information.
* Analyze recruiter emails for authenticity.
* Detect common recruitment scam patterns.
* Allow users to report fraudulent recruiters and companies.
* Protect users from sharing sensitive information with unverified recruiters.
* Provide transparent AI explanations instead of simple yes/no decisions.

# 11. Risks and Limitations

* AI predictions are probabilistic and may produce false positives or false negatives.
* Company verification depends on publicly available information.
* Some scams may evade automated detection.
* Continuous updates are required as scam techniques evolve.

The platform is designed to assist users in decision-making and should not be considered a legal authority or a definitive fraud detection service.

---

# 12. Conclusion

JobShield aims to provide a secure, intelligent, and scalable solution for combating online recruitment fraud. By integrating Artificial Intelligence with company verification, recruiter validation, and community reporting, the platform helps job seekers make informed decisions while promoting safer recruitment practices.

The project also demonstrates modern full-stack software engineering practices, secure application development, AI integration, and real-world problem solving, making it suitable for deployment as an industry-level application.
